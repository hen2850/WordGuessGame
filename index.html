Word Guess Game – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 19 April 2025

Thank you for choosing Word Guess Game (the “App”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what limited information we handle, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

1. 100 % Offline Experience

The App is designed to run entirely offline on your device. It does not:

  • connect to the internet or external servers;
  • require or request an account, login, or personal details;
  • share data with third parties.

2. Data We Handle

Category What Where it lives Why we keep it
Gameplay Statistics Number of games played, wins/losses, guesses per game, fastest win time On‑device only (inside your iPhone/iPad secure storage) To let you track your performance and progress

No other data—personal or technical—is collected by the App.

Apple‑Provided, Opt‑In Analytics

Apple may collect aggregated, anonymous diagnostics and usage statistics on your device if you have opted into iOS “Share with App Developers.” We do not see, store, or control this information. Learn more in Apple’s own Analytics & Privacy documentation.

3. Data Sharing

We never transmit or share your gameplay statistics or any other data with:

  • third‑party analytics platforms;
  • advertisers or ad networks;
  • social media services;
  • cloud backups or servers controlled by us.

4. Security

Because all data remains on your device, its protection relies on the security features of iOS/iPadOS, including device passcodes, Face ID/Touch ID, and on‑device encryption.

6. Your Choices

  • Remove the App – Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.
  • Analytics Opt‑Out – Disable “Share with App Developers” in iOS Settings to stop Apple from sending anonymous analytics.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how we handle data—for example, by adding online leaderboards—we will:

  1. Update this Privacy Policy;
  2. Publish the new version in‑App and on our App Store page;
  3. Clearly highlight the changes before they take effect.

8. Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach us at hen2850@hotmail.com.

Play, guess, and relax—your data never leaves your phone.
